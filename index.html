De Me

A Passion for the Classics,
A Commitment to Community

✦

Salvete! I'm Aahil Hasan, and JCL has been my home since 2023, when I joined as an 8th grader. This year I will attend my 4th State Convention and 4th National Convention, and I want to spend the next year giving back to the organization that has given me so much.

I founded and captained the Latin League chapter at my school (Regional champions, 2 years running!). I've competed on the National Certamen circuit for three years, two as captain, and served as Co-President of the Shrewsbury NJCL Latin Honor Society two years running.

As MassJCL Treasurer (2024-2025) and unofficial fundraising coordinator the year before, I managed over $25,000 in funds, introduced Zeffy to the organization, revived our 501(c)(3) nonprofit status, and launched fundraisers raising more than $5,000 in a single year for scholarships and convention cost reductions.

Outside of JCL, I'm an officer in Speech & Debate, Student Health and Wellness Advisory Committee board, Language Advisory Committee Board, a 2nd degree black belt in Taekwondo, and a lead fundraiser in both Robotics and Crew. I have made some of my best friends in this organization, and it would mean the world to me to serve it as your President.

Platform

SALVE

My platform, one letter at a time.

S State & Local Outreach

Strengthening Connections Across the Circuit

I want to start idea exchanges with local JCLs to share what works and build a stronger state network. I will create a resource timeline and peer-mentorship system so schools starting new chapters have real guidance. We (huge shoutout to Mrs. Sinacola!) grew Shrewsbury JCL from a solo certamen player to ten delegates at this year's state convention; We know how hard that climb is.

A Alumni Connections

Anchoring Our Community to Its Roots

I built a spreadsheet of MassJCL alumni and engaged them for Giving Tuesday and they gave generously. As president, I will launch exclusive alumni newsletters to keep former members informed, connected, and invested in the future of the organization they once called home.

L Listening to Ideas

Leading with Open Ears

I want every member to have a real way to share feedback, positive or constructive. I will add a standing feedback form to the president's email signature so anyone, from any chapter, can reach leadership easily. Your voice shapes this organization.

V Vision for Fundraising & Service

Vaulting MassJCL's Impact Forward

As treasurer and fundraising coordinator, I launched Chipotle Dine-Ins, a Silent Auction, Giving Tuesday, and grocery gift card drives, raising over $5,000 in a year for scholarships and convention support. I will keep that momentum going, explore read-a-thons and walk-a-thons, and introduce accessible service projects like letter-writing campaigns for people in hospitals.

E Entertainment

Energizing MassJCL with That's Entertainment

Let's bring back the beloved That's Entertainment showcase, a night where students from across the state perform, laugh, and celebrate together. Having written every officer skit this year, from Nationals to Kickoff to Classics Day, I know how to craft moments that bring a room alive.

+ Additional Priorities

More on the Agenda

Launch a Career Fair connecting classics to real-world paths. Publish "How to Run Your Chapter" guides for new and growing JCLs. Expand chapter enrollment by enlisting new schools. And always center the experience of every member, whether a seasoned Certamen veteran or first-time attendee.
